FAQs

How do I place a bid ?

After selecting the items you are interested in bidding for, you will input your bid in the field provided in the description of the item. N.B. You are not allowed to place bids below the starting bid of any item.

How do I know I have successfully placed a bid ?

Immediately your bid value has been inputted for an item, it moves to your cart indicating your bid has been placed. You will also receive email notification for bids successful bids placed any interested item.

Can I place bids for more than one item ?

You are allowed to place bids for more than one item, however the bids must be placed separately for each item. N.B. Please note you are not allowed to place multiple bids for a single item. Placing multiple bids for the same item disqualifies you from getting your bids accepted even if one of your bids is the highest for that item.

How do I know if my Bid is accepted ?

Upon successful bidding for an item, you will receive feedback on the status of your bid on the same email notification sent when your bid was placed. This feedback will state if your bid is accepted as the highest for the item and also clarify if you aren’t. The highest bid will be stated and your position compared to the winner will also be stated.
